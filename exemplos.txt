Q: Quais usuários ainda não devolveram os livros que pegaram emprestados?
Q: Which users haven't returned the books they borrowed?
A: SELECT usuarios.nome FROM usuarios
   JOIN emprestimos ON emprestimos.usuario_id = usuarios.id
   WHERE emprestimos.data_devolucao IS NULL;

Q: Quais usuários ainda não devolveram os itens emprestados?
Q: Which users haven't returned their borrowed items?
A: SELECT u.nome FROM usuarios u
   JOIN emprestimos e ON e.usuario_id = u.id
   WHERE e.data_devolucao IS NULL;

Q: Qual a categoria mais frequente?
Q: What is the most frequent category?
A: SELECT categoria, COUNT(*) AS total FROM itens
   GROUP BY categoria ORDER BY total DESC LIMIT 1;

Q: Qual a categoria menos frequente?
Q: What is the least frequent category?
A: SELECT categoria, COUNT(*) AS total FROM itens
   GROUP BY categoria ORDER BY total ASC LIMIT 1;

Q: Qual o item mais emprestado?
Q: What is the most borrowed item?
A: SELECT i.nome, COUNT(*) AS total FROM itens i
   JOIN emprestimos e ON e.item_id = i.id
   GROUP BY i.nome ORDER BY total DESC LIMIT 1;

Q: Quais itens nunca foram emprestados?
Q: List all items that have never been borrowed?
A: SELECT i.nome FROM itens i
   LEFT JOIN emprestimos e ON e.item_id = i.id
   WHERE e.id IS NULL;

Q: Quais usuários nunca fizeram nenhum registro?
Q: List all users that have never made any record?
A: SELECT u.nome FROM usuarios u
   LEFT JOIN emprestimos e ON e.usuario_id = u.id
   WHERE e.id IS NULL;

Q: List all distinct values in a column?

Q: Qual o nome e a contagem agrupados por relacionamento?
Q: Get the name and count grouped by a foreign key relation?
A: SELECT b.nome, COUNT(*) AS total FROM tabela_a a
   JOIN tabela_b b ON b.id = a.b_id
   GROUP BY b.nome ORDER BY total DESC;

Q: Quais registros têm data entre dois valores?
Q: Which records have a date between two values?

Q: Which items appear more than once?
